Our Construction Services

Residential Construction

We build dream homes from the ground up. Whether it's a single-story unit or a luxury villa, our team manages the entire lifecycle of the build, ensuring a solid foundation and a flawless finish tailored to your family's needs.

Commercial Developments

We deliver robust commercial infrastructures including office buildings, retail spaces, and warehouses. Our commercial projects focus on optimizing space, ensuring structural longevity, and meeting all regulatory standards.

Renovation & Remodeling

Transform your existing space with our renovation services. From structural modifications to complete aesthetic overhauls, we breathe new life into older buildings, enhancing their value and functionality.

Project Management

We take the stress out of building. Our comprehensive project management covers budgeting, scheduling, material procurement, and site supervision, ensuring your project is delivered on time and within budget.

Architectural Planning

Our collaboration with top architects ensures that your vision is translated into practical blueprints. We provide structural drawings, 3D modeling, and council approval assistance to streamline the pre-construction phase.

Steel Structures & Roofing

Specializing in durable steel fabrications and roofing solutions for both industrial and residential needs. We ensure weather-proofing, thermal insulation, and long-lasting structural stability.

Maintenance & Repairs

Construction doesn't end at handover. We offer ongoing maintenance services, structural repairs, and waterproofing solutions to protect your investment for decades to come.

Interior & Exterior Finishing

From masonry and plastering to painting and tiling, our finishing teams ensure that the final look of your building reflects the high quality of the structure underneath.

Landscaping & Paving

Complete your project with our exterior services. We design and construct driveways, retaining walls, and landscape features that complement your new building perfectly.

Frequently Asked Questions

While we are based in Gampaha, Trandy Construction operates throughout the Western Province and surrounding areas. For large-scale commercial projects, we are available to work island-wide across Sri Lanka. Please contact us to discuss your specific location.

Yes, we provide end-to-end support. Our team assists with preparing architectural drawings, structural certificates, and submitting necessary documents to local councils (Pradeshiya Sabha/Urban Council) to obtain building permits and approvals smoothly.

We offer flexible contract models. We can work on a "Labor Only" basis where you supply materials, or a "Turnkey" basis where we handle both materials and labor. We generally recommend Turnkey solutions as it allows us to control quality and timeline better, but we are happy to discuss your preference.

We source materials only from reputable suppliers and manufacturers in Sri Lanka. Whether it's cement, steel, or finishing items, we verify the quality against industry standards. We are also transparent with our clients and can provide material samples for approval before installation.

The timeline depends on the size, design complexity, and weather conditions. Typically, a standard two-story residential project takes between 6 to 10 months to complete from foundation to handover. We provide a detailed schedule before starting any project.
